Why do we exist?

Live Commerce has a problem with data

Even though conversion rates and sales figures are sometimes up to double that of normal eCommerce benchmarks, repeat purchase from Live Commerce shoppers is extremely low.

The problem is...

Live Commerce has gown so quickly that brands and streamers don’t know what best practice looks like. Without access to the right data, they are left stabbing in the dark trying to optimize their efforts.
